## Summary Observations

**Most crowded categories:** AI content creation, AI coding agents, and AI agent infrastructure dominate. These three account for roughly 30 products combined and suffer from extreme similarity.

**Highest traction products:**
1. WURK (@WURKDOTFUN) — 19 likes — AI agents hiring humans via crypto micro-tasks
2. The Webb (@Thewebb_io) — 16 likes — unclear product, but highest raw engagement
3. EVDC (@evdc_network) — 15 likes — EV charger passive income network
4. SureThing (@getsurething) — 4 likes — AI employee with persistent memory
5. goAsk (@goAskapp) — 3 likes — pay-per-response creator monetization

**Pattern:** The products with the most engagement tend to be the ones with the clearest, most tangible value proposition — "earn money from your parking space," "get paid for expert answers." The generic "AI-powered X" products cluster at zero likes regardless of category.

**Red flags:** ~40% of products had zero or near-zero descriptive content, suggesting many are pre-launch or side projects posted speculatively. The AI agent/coding/content trifecta shows classic gold-rush crowding with minimal differentiation.
