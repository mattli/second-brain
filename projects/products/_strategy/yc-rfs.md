# YC Request for Startups

Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/rfs

## Spring 2026

1. **Cursor for Product Managers** — AI-native system for product discovery: synthesizing user interviews, usage data, and feedback to answer "what should we build next?" and generate feature specs ready for coding agents.

2. **AI-Native Hedge Funds** — Funds built ground-up on AI, not bolting AI onto existing strategies. Swarms of agents combing 10-Ks, earnings calls, SEC filings, and synthesizing trades.

3. **AI-Native Agencies** — Service businesses with software margins. Use AI internally to deliver finished work (design, ads, legal docs) at scale, rather than selling software to clients.

4. **Stablecoin Financial Services** — Financial services layer built on stablecoins: yield-bearing accounts, tokenized real-world assets, cross-border payments — compliant but crypto-native.

5. **AI for Government** — Tools to help local, state, and federal government process the coming flood of AI-generated form submissions and applications. Sticky contracts, huge upside.

6. **Modern Metal Mills** — Software-defined American metal mills (aluminum, steel) with AI-driven planning, real-time MES, and modern automation to compress 8–30 week lead times.

7. **AI Guidance for Physical Work** — Real-time AI coaching for field workers via phone/glasses/AirPods. See what the worker sees, guide them through the job. Addresses skilled labor shortage.

8. **Large Spatial Models** — Foundation models for spatial reasoning — geometry, 3D structure, physical manipulation — enabling AI to understand and design real-world objects and environments.

9. **Infra for Government Fraud Hunters** — Tools for whistleblower law firms and inspectors general to accelerate False Claims Act cases: parsing PDFs, tracing corporate structures, building complaint-ready files.

10. **Make LLMs Easy to Train** — Better tooling for LLM training: APIs that abstract training, large dataset management, ML-native dev environments. Post-training and specialization becoming critical.
