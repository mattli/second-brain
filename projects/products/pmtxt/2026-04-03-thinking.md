# pmtxt — Latest Thinking

## Product Shape

- PM builds a structured knowledge base (customers, competitors, goals, constraints, revenue model)
- Feeds in signals (feature requests, interview notes, research)
- AI evaluates against full context, recommends what to build with grounded reasoning
- Output is lightweight — feature description + rationale, enough to hand off to a coding tool
- Context compounds over time, making recommendations better
- Sits upstream of Cursor/Claude Code, not competing with them
